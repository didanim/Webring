# Personal Website Webring

A small webring for personal websites, blogs, portfolios, and digital gardens.

The goal of this webring is to help independent websites discover one another and encourage visitors to explore beyond social media and large platforms.

## What is a Webring?

A webring is a collection of websites connected through a shared navigation widget. Visitors can move from one site to another, creating a chain of discovery among like-minded creators.

## Requirements

To join the webring, your website must:

- Be publicly accessible.
- Have the webring widget embedded somewhere on your site.
- Contain original content.
- Not contain malicious software, spam, or phishing content.
- Clearly indicate if mature content (NSFW) is present.

## How to Join

1. Add the webring widget to your website.
2. Open a new **Site Submission** issue.
3. Fill out all required information.
4. Wait for your submission to be reviewed.

## Submission Information

Please provide:

- Website URL
- SFW or NSFW rating
- Banner image or GIF URL
- URL of the page where the widget is embedded
- Short description of your website

## 🔗 Webring Widget

### NavLink Ads

You can replace `width=180` or `height=180` to your liking.

```html
<iframe frameborder='0' height='180' name='sitelinks' src='https://didanim.github.io/Webring/widget.html' width='180'></iframe>
```

### Samyang Lovers
Replace the `yoursitename` based on your website URL.
```html
<iframe frameborder='0' height='180' src='https://didanim.github.io/Webring/samyang-webring/widget.html?site=yoursitename' width='180'></iframe>
```

## Rules

- Keep your website active.
- Notify the maintainer if your URL changes.
- Do not remove the widget after joining.
- Sites may be removed if they become inactive for an extended period.
- Respect fellow members.

## Why Join?

- Discover new personal websites.
- Support independent creators.
- Share your blog, portfolio, or digital space.
- Be part of a community-driven web.
