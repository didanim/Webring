Welcome to Webring

A rotating webring for indie personal websites. Join the network and help fellow webmasters discover each other's sites.

How It Works

  1. Submit your website and square banner.
  2. Wait for approval.
  3. Embed the widget on your site.
  4. Your website joins the rotation.

About

This project works like a mini ad exchange for personal websites. Every approved site gets shown throughout the network.

Requirements

  • Square image
  • 180×180 to 360×360
  • Maximum 1MB
  • Widget must be embedded
